About Shalom Primary Hospital

Shalom Primary Hospital, located in Mekelle, Tigray, Ethiopia, is committed to providing quality healthcare services to the community. Our dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and staff ensure patients receive compassionate care and advanced medical attention.

Founded by Dr. Kinfe, the hospital has grown to become a trusted medical center for families, patients, and the community at large.

Shalom Primary Hospital is a healthcare institution located in Mekelle, Tigray, Ethiopia. The hospital is dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality medical services through a team of committed healthcare professionals.

Our mission is to improve the health and well-being of our community by delivering ethical, patient-centered care in a safe and respectful environment.
